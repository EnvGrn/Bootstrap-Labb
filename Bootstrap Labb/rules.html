Rules That Hogwarts Students Must Follow

In the world of Harry Potter, turning eleven marks a child’s coming of age when they receive an acceptance letter from Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
Receiving this letter also marks the moment a future witch or wizard's entire world turns upside down.
The moment they step through those giant doors, any logic they’ve learned up to that point goes out the window.

Just like any school, Hogwarts has a strict set of rules that students must obey. Some of these rules have been in place for decades, existing since the school was founded.


1. Violence Between Students Is Only Permitted In A Duel

2. Always Respect Curfew

3. No Entering Other House Dormitories

4. Do Not Enter The Forbidden Forest

5. The Students' Belongings Must Be Searched For Contraband

6. Students Under 17 Are Constantly Observed

7. No Reading From The Restricted Section

8. Pointed Hats And Proper Attire Are Required

9. Butterbeer Is Not Allowed On School Grounds

10. It Is Forbidden To Approach The Whomping Willow

11. It Is Forbidden To Cheat By Way Of Magic

12. The Use And Brewing Of Dangerous Potions Is Forbidden

13. Cursed Objects Are Banned

14. All Professors Must Be Respected
